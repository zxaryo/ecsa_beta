About ECSA

The Electronics and Communication Students' Association functions with an objective to organize various activities that nourishes the academic and professional development of students along with leadership qualities, teamwork and other essential employability skills.

The association conducts events like workshops, career development programmes and talks from eminent persons from industry to bridge the gap between the industry and academics. The ECSA fosters personal growth, leadership, and opportunities in Science, Technology.ECSA provides the platforms for students to prove their skills through many activities.
